VISAP - Fisheries Advisory Service

Introduction

This application is a viewer of the data collected by ICATMAR to manage fishing on the coast of Catalonia. Currently the application only shows data from the sampling that has been done in trawl fishing.

The ICATMAR (Catalan Research Institute for the Governance of the Sea) is a cooperation body between the Directorate General of Fisheries and Maritime Affairs and the Institute of Marine Sciences (ICM-CSIC) with management autonomy, born from the need for scientific advice to develop the Catalan Maritime Strategy programme.

A team made up of technicians specialized in marine biological sciences and in computer tools for geolocation and information support, coordinated and advised by researchers from the Renewable Resources Department of the Barcelona Institute of Marine Sciences, have been the material authors of the scientific monitoring that has been aimed at maritime fisheries in both professional and recreational aspects.

In October 2018, the monitoring program for the species of greatest fishing interest was launched, where one of the key elements is the collection of data and its structuring so that it can be analyzed and visualized.

Funding

This application has been developed in the framework of the Xarxa Marítima de Catalunya (BlueNetCat) , which is financed by the Generalitat de Catalunya and the European Regional Development Fund (FEDER).
